## WHERE 절
### WHERE OR 조건 여러개 --> IN 으로 묶기
```sql
SELECT
   FROM
   WHERE JOB = 'MANAGER'
      OR JOB = 'SALESMAN'
      OR JOB = 'CLERK';
```
```SQL
SELECT
   FROM
   WHERE JOB IN ('MANAGER','SALESMAN','CLERK');
```
